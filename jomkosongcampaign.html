Tealive has pledged to provide one million free school meals through its "Jom Kosong" campaign, supporting the Ministry of Domestic Trade and Cost of Living's initiative to promote zero-sugar options. For each cup sold under this campaign, 20 sen will be donated to the Tealive School Meals Fund, aiming to raise RM3.5 million to fund one million meals. The campaign officially began on December 1, 2024.
