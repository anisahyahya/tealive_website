About Tealive

Tealive is Southeast Asia’s leading lifestyle tea brand, boasting over 1,000 outlets worldwide. Established in Malaysia in 2017, Tealive was born from a passion for bringing modern tea culture to the world.

With a dynamic spirit of innovation, we continue to expand strategically, offering a diverse menu that includes signature milk teas, refreshing smoothies, indulgent chocolate drinks, and aromatic coffee blends.

Serving over five million customers monthly in Malaysia alone, Tealive has made its mark in international markets such as the Philippines, Vietnam, Myanmar, Cambodia, Brunei, Mauritius, and Canada—soon extending its reach to the UAE.
