TEALIVE ECO STRAWLESS CUP

What is the Eco Strawless Cup Campaign?

The Eco Strawless Cup Campaign is an initiative by Tealive to promote sustainability by introducing an innovative cup design that eliminates the need for plastic straws. The cup features a built-in compartment for toppings, allowing consumers to enjoy their drinks more conveniently while reducing plastic waste.

What is the purpose of the campaign?

The main goal of the campaign is to reduce plastic waste, particularly single-use plastic straws, and encourage more environmentally friendly consumption habits. Through this initiative, Tealive aims to raise awareness about sustainability and provide an eco-friendly alternative without compromising the drinking experience.

The campaign starts on August 30, 2024.
