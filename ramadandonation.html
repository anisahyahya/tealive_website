Ramadan Donation Campaign

Helping the underprivileged during Ramadan and Hari Raya

What is the Ramadan Donation Campaign?

The Ramadan Donation Campaign is a charity initiative to help underprivileged Muslims during Ramadan and in preparation for Hari Raya. This campaign provides food, financial aid, and other essential support to the poor, orphans, and those in need.

Organized by Tealive, this campaign allows customers to contribute by adding a donation to their purchase. Every contribution helps bring joy and relief to those struggling during this holy month.
